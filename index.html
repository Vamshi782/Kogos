Most Indians follow the crowd to USA or UK - spending years in visa queues, cap lotteries, and uncertainty. Europe's fastest visa takes 2 weeks. Most people never knew this was an option.

A pharmacist earning Rs 30K/month in India earns EUR 65 Lakhs in Netherlands for the exact same work. An engineer stuck in H-1B limbo earns less than their European counterpart - with far less stability.

Our Story

Why KOGOS Exists

We built the resource we wish had existed when we were trying to figure out where to go, what visa to get, and which country would actually value our skills.

"The question was never should I go abroad. The question was where - and nobody had a real answer."

When I started looking at moving abroad, I found endless YouTube videos promoting the same old destinations - USA, UK - with no real comparison of which country would actually pay for my specific background, accept my visa category, or have companies that hire Indians.

I spent months researching what a simple resource could have told me in 15 minutes. So I built that resource. For pharma professionals looking at Netherlands. For engineers considering Germany. For anyone who wants honest information before making a life-changing decision.

KOGOS is that community - where the information is free, the guidance is honest, and every Indian abroad can find their path.
