escape routes
4 PATHS OUT.
PICK YOURS.
01
Direct Job Application
Fastest — 3 to 6 months
Apply directly to European tech companies. Get an offer. Visa follows automatically in 2–4 weeks. Best for engineers with 3+ years experience. Netherlands and Ireland are your easiest entry points — both English-speaking workplaces, both fast visa processes.
02
Masters First — Then Get Hired
Safest — 18 to 24 months
Come on a student visa. Do a 1–2 year Masters in CS or Data Science at a German or Dutch university — tuition near €0. During your thesis, intern at a European tech company. Graduate with EU degree + EU experience. Companies hire you as a local candidate. Success rate: 10x higher than applying from India.
03
Indian IT Firm in Europe
Easiest Entry Point
Get transferred by your Indian IT company — TCS, Infosys, Wipro, HCL, Cognizant all have large European offices. Request a transfer to their Netherlands, Germany or Ireland delivery center. Internal transfer bypasses job hunting entirely. Once inside Europe for 2 years, job hop to a local European company for 3x salary.
04
EU Digital Nomad Visa → Convert
New in 2024–2026
Germany, Portugal, Spain, and Czech Republic now offer Digital Nomad visas. Move to Europe working remotely for an Indian company. Build your network on the ground. Convert to a local work visa when you land a European employer. Start networking in-person — Europeans hire people they have met.

positioning guide
HOW TO POSITION
YOURSELF TO GET HIRED
1
Update LinkedIn Headline — Do This Today
Dutch and German recruiters search LinkedIn first. If your headline doesn't say visa status — they skip you assuming complex immigration.
"Senior Data Engineer | Python Spark AWS | 5 Yrs | Open to Netherlands & Germany | EU Blue Card / HSM Visa Eligible"
2
Remove These From Your CV Immediately
Photo, Date of Birth, Marital Status, Objective Statement, References. European CVs: 1–2 pages max, ATS-friendly, no tables. Save as PDF.
Filename: FirstName_LastName_SWE_CV_2026.pdf
3
Create a XING Profile — Germans Use This
XING is Germany's LinkedIn. German companies check XING before LinkedIn. Create your profile at xing.com with the exact same content as LinkedIn. Most Indian engineers miss this completely.
xing.com → Create profile → Mirror your LinkedIn exactly
4
Apply on Career Page + Email HR Same Day
Apply on the official career page. Same day — find the Engineering Recruiter or TA Manager on LinkedIn. Send a 3-line email. Doing both doubles your callback rate.
5
Mention Visa Upfront — Never Hide It
Say it in line 1 of your email: "I am eligible for the EU Blue Card / Highly Skilled Migrant Visa — straightforward process, no burden on your team." This removes their only hesitation instantly.
6
Set LinkedIn Job Alerts — Be First to Apply
80% of applications come in the first 48 hours. Set daily alerts for "Software Engineer Netherlands", "Data Engineer Germany", "ML Engineer Ireland". Apply within 24 hours of every posting.
LinkedIn → Jobs → Set Alert → Daily → Your role + Country
