Your 5-step roadmap to get there
1
Update your CV to European format
No photo. No date of birth. No marital status. 1–2 pages max. Use action verbs. Quantify every achievement. Use Europass template as a starting point.
2
Optimise your LinkedIn headline
Write: "QA Analyst | GMP | M.Pharm | Open to Netherlands & Ireland | HSM Visa Eligible" — recruiters in Netherlands search LinkedIn before job boards.
3
Apply on company careers page directly
Not Indeed. Not Naukri. Go to careers.roche.com, novartis.com/careers, iconplc.com/careers directly. Apply there first.
4
Cold email HR on same day you apply
Find the Talent Acquisition Manager or HR Business Partner on LinkedIn. Email them directly with 3 lines — your background, your visa status, and a request for a 20-minute call.
5
Mention visa upfront — never hide it
Say "I am eligible for the Highly Skilled Migrant Visa — no complex process on your end" in your first email. This removes their biggest fear immediately and sets you apart.
