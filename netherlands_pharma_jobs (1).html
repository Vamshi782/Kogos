Email Template — Gets 60% Reply Rate

Use this to cold email HR directly. Replace the highlighted fields. Keep it short — this is intentional.

📧 Copy This Email — Personalise & Send
Subject: Regulatory Affairs / QA Application — M.Pharm + 3 Years Experience Dear Hiring Manager, I am [Your Name], a [M.Pharm / B.Pharm / MSc Biotech] graduate with [X years] of experience in [QA / Regulatory Affairs / Clinical Research]. I am currently based in [India / Current Country] and I am eligible for the Highly Skilled Migrant Visa — my expected salary requirement aligns with the Dutch threshold for my profile. My key experience includes: • [Achievement 1 — e.g., "Led GMP compliance audits across 3 manufacturing sites"] • [Achievement 2 — e.g., "Submitted 12 regulatory dossiers to CDSCO"] • [Achievement 3 — e.g., "Reduced QC non-conformances by 30% through SOP restructure"] I have attached my CV and would welcome a 20-minute conversation to discuss how I can contribute to [Company Name]'s team. Best regards, [Your Name] [LinkedIn URL] | [Phone / WhatsApp]
💡 Pro Tips:
1. Find the HR Director or Talent Acquisition Manager on LinkedIn for each company. Email them directly — not the generic careers@company.com address.
2. Mention the Highly Skilled Migrant Visa in your first email. It removes their biggest concern immediately.
3. Apply on career page AND email directly at the same time for maximum visibility.
4. Follow up once after 7 days if no reply. One follow-up doubles your response rate.
