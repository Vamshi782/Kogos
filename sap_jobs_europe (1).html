💼
jobs.sap.com
SAP's own official careers portal. Always check here first — internal roles, global openings, and partner listings. Filter by Europe → your country → your module.

LinkedIn Jobs
Search: "SAP FICO Consultant" → filter by Germany / Netherlands / Ireland. Set a Job Alert — you get notified the moment a role is posted. Message the recruiter directly after applying.

Indeed Europe
Use your exact module keyword — "SAP S/4HANA", "SAP MM", "SAP FICO". Add "visa sponsorship" to the keyword. Search by country-specific Indeed (de.indeed.com for Germany, nl.indeed.com for Netherlands).

XING
Germany's version of LinkedIn. Massive for SAP roles in DACH region (Germany, Austria, Switzerland). Create a profile here — German recruiters use XING more than LinkedIn. Many Indians miss this completely.

Top job board in Germany and Austria. Thousands of SAP listings. Filter by "Berufserfahrung" (experience level) and look for companies offering relocation. Most listings are in English too.

Good for SAP roles AND salary benchmarking. Read company reviews before you apply. Filter "Visa Sponsorship" in benefits. Also useful to research interview questions for each company.

Cold Email Template for SAP Roles

Email the SAP Practice Lead or IT Recruitment Manager directly on LinkedIn. Do not use generic careers@ email addresses.

📧 Copy, Personalise & Send
Subject: SAP [Your Module] Consultant — [X Years] Experience | Open to Relocation to [Country] Dear [Hiring Manager Name], I am [Your Name], an SAP [FICO / MM / SD / S4HANA / ABAP] Consultant with [X years] of experience across [industry, e.g., Manufacturing / FMCG / Pharma]. I am actively exploring opportunities in [Germany / Netherlands / Ireland] and am eligible for the Highly Skilled Migrant / EU Blue Card Visa — no sponsorship complexity on your end. Key highlights: • [e.g., Led full-cycle S/4HANA implementation for 3 business units — go-live in 6 months] • [e.g., SAP FICO configuration for 5 company codes across India and Middle East] • [e.g., Certified in SAP S/4HANA Finance — 2023] I would welcome a 20-minute call to discuss how I can contribute to [Company Name]'s SAP roadmap. Best regards, [Your Name] [LinkedIn URL] | [Phone / WhatsApp]
💡 SAP-Specific Tips:
1. Always mention your specific module in the subject line — "SAP Consultant" is too vague and gets ignored.
2. Mention your S/4HANA experience even if partial — every company is migrating right now and this keyword gets you callbacks.
3. Germany uses XING more than LinkedIn for SAP roles. Create a XING profile with the same content as LinkedIn.
4. Add "EU Blue Card eligible" or "Highly Skilled Migrant Visa eligible" to your LinkedIn headline — recruiters filter for this.
